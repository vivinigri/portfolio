About

Formatch brings an innovative gameplay to the world of matching games that will guarantee great fun for all ages. The mechanics are really easy to learn but you will have to develop your own strategies to collect the most shapes. If you like challenging games, this game is exactly for you. Take a risk in hard mode or in Zen mode.

Your goal here is to eliminate as many forms as possible from the entire stack.(preferably all) To do this - you must combine each of the shapes with all of its correspondents in horizontal or vertical lines.

Each shape needs different amounts of elements in the deck - and you need to gather them all together to remove them. The balls only need 2 to be removed, the triangles 3, the squares 4 and the pentagon 5. Thus, you must rush and deftly calculate the ideal place to position the each shape.

The game has a wonderful tactic, but one that should be used very carefully - the possibility of "crowding" pieces of the grid in one direction through a swipe. This way, you may be able to close combinations of shapes that seemed to be impossible, but be careful, as it could end up generating even more mess ...

Keep on challenging yourself to surpass your highscore and share it with your friends.

Download Formatch now and find out how fun this puzzle-strategy game is.
