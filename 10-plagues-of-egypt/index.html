About

Travel back in time to when the plagues were devastating Egypt.

Help Moses with all your strategy and speed in 10 different mini games.

Features:

  • 10 different mini games and levels
  • Two game modes: arcade & zen
  • Achievements to collect
  • Hexagonal game design
  • Retro arcade graphics
  • Share your score with friends
  • Dayeinu 8 bits version!
  • Learn the Torah passages of the plagues
